what is this mod?

This is a Modification using "Dark Ages Weapon Pack" by TheCatMask as a base.

- the mod is meant to be a Gameplay mod / Zandronum DM mod : hence the name "Dark Ages : Deathmatch"
(Check Credits for more info)
=== WEAPONS ===

Shield Saw: 
 Is included in all the weapons, via altfire (Block) Reload (Toss), and
 can be used by itself as it's own weapon, that the player starts with.
 
 If you block with a projectile with your shield in the Perry state,
 you can reflect projectiles and cause the shield to enter into a "shield Deflect"
 state that summons devastating fireballs.(works with player projectiles as well)
 
  
  While blocking, if you press Fire, the shield will preform a "Shield Dash"
  "Shield Dash" Propels you forward and does impact damage to enemies.
  
Dark Mace:
  The dark mace is a powerful Melee weapon that replaces the Chainsaw Slot
  it has a powerful crushing blow that eliminates enemies in a radius.
  when picked up, it also upgrades your "Flail" or Zoom State.
  
  (The "Flail" is your Melee Attack tied to the Reload key.)
  
Shredder:
   Fires weak nails, it is your pistol weapon.
   
Shotgun: 
   Players start with this weapon as well, this weapon fires a vertical spread
   of pellets.

Super Shotgun:
   The lever action double barreled shotgun that does devastating damage to your
   foes.
   
Pulverizer:
   Your 'chaingun', it crushes skulls and uses the remaining shrapnel as ammunition.
   
Accelerator:
   The Accelerator is the plasma rifle of the mod, it fires rapid bolts of plasma.
 
ChainShot:
   The ChainShot is an energy Ball and Chain that destroyed players into a pulp, and
   can not be parried or blocked!

BFC:
  the 'BIG FUCKING CROSSBOW', is a energy crossbow that fires BFG plasma bolts
  with the BFG tracers and damage!
